— The Lounge —

Slow down.
Stay a while.

Coupe & Stem is a small neighborhood lounge built for the good, unhurried hours — the kind of place you walk into on a Wednesday and end up staying till close. Think low couches and warm lamplight, a leafy green canopy overhead, and a bartender who remembers your order.

No velvet ropes, no thumping playlist. Just a comfortable room, an easy menu of drinks done well, and a little corner of the Redlands that feels like it was made for regulars.

  • Couches, lounge chairs & high-tops
  • Candlelit, plant-filled room
  • Simple menu, honest pours
  • A true neighborhood spot
